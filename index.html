The Origin

The Lore of CyberBit

The battle between Good and Bad never stops. It is continued from past centuries till now. Evil powers come in different forms to rule over the earth. To crush their evil motives, good souls always come forward. But this time, it will not be easy. The evil powers are back with their evil intention in this 21st century.

15th Century — The Dark Origin

A great wizard of evil mind rises to power. His dark arts transcend centuries, his soul escaping his mortal body. His body now rests on the island of Zinto, which both exudes and is filled with magic.

The Emerald Blood War

So titled because of the greenish hue of a printed circuit board versus what is essentially the most basic symbol of life — blood. Though the conflicting interpretations of the title vary, war is war.

Year 2100 — The Augmentation Era

Companies reach the peak of a human modification revolution. People with enough currency could buy augmentations, additions, improvements. But greed divides them, and nations begin to collapse.

21st Century — CyberBit Rises

Xin from Faspra City is ready to fight against his archfoe Tzin. With special electro-magnetic powers (cyberware), CyberBit battles against the crime and evil creatures to save the earth.

♥ Heart (Xin)
Mind (Tzin) ☠
Xin means Heart. In our daily life, we face hard situations where we must listen to our heart to take decisions. Heart is considered as pure — and so our protagonist is named Xin. He represents the courage of pure decisions, the strength of compassion, the power of science wielded with a good soul.

The Story Begins

Issue #01 — Teaser

Birth of a Hero

Finally, it is Sunday. All of his friends were free and had bought all the stuff he had told them to buy. They arrived at Xin's home at around 3:00pm and as they were arriving they started catching up about their college life.


"Yeah, it's great up there. I'm meeting a lot of girls"

"But I guess you're not having luck with any of them or you wouldn't be here, right?"

Everyone laughed. Those were Ryan and Charlie...


But one of them got past him and was going straight for Charlie. Xin turned around, reached out to try and catch it. He was far away from reaching it, but he could feel an energy surge flowing through his body. He focused on the palm of his hand and he let out a scream... A wave of energy was expelled from his body and reached the insect, which fell down after a tiny explosion due to an evident short-circuit.


All of them looked at Xin, in absolute awe. But no one was as surprised as Xin himself.

Issue #02 — Teaser

Rise of a Hero

After the last incident, Xin woke up with a nightmare, feeling exhausted, nauseous, and with a headache the likes of which he hadn't felt since the last time he got drunk, 7 months before.


"Hey baby, got some sleep?" said his mother.

"Well, I heard you arriving at around 6:30am and its 10 o'clock right now so... Almost 30 hours! That must be a new record or something!"


The Professor's face popped through the door. His evident lack of sleep, plus his head full of gray and his unkempt gray beard would've made anyone think this half a century old man was approaching his 70th birthday.


Tyro knew what could happen after the incident at the lab, and that had him terrified. He knew that the teenagers weren't in danger, at least at the moment, but he knew they would never be the same again. The professor decided his main objective would be to think of a way to control the explosion effects.
